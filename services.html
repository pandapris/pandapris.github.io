Services

General Pest Control

General pest control include spraying, fogging, baiting, and trapping. Treatment for general pest control covers pest like cockroaches, ants, flies, mosquitoes, fleas, bedbug, rodents, etc. The chemicals used for a general pest control spray are totally safe for humans and pets that shall be those approved by Public Health insecticides as approved by the Pesticides Board of Malaysia.

During spraying and fogging, it is recommended that any pet food and water bowls are removed prior to treatment and cleaned after treatment. Food and water must also be covered. Fish and reptile pets must also be remove before spraying and fogging because the chemical may harm them.

Proven Termites Baiting System

Termite baiting is to place a food source that contains toxicant. Foraging termites that find the baits will consume and share the food containing the toxicant with its nest mates including the Queen and the King through a process called trophallaxis. Elimination process may require approximately 2 to 12 weeks depending on the size of the colony, typically 6 to 8 weeks. There will be two types of baiting system that we use which are In-Ground Baiting System which for preventing future invading colony from infesting the premises and Above Ground Baiting System which is to eliminate current termite infestation in the premises.

Pre- & Post-Construction Anti Termites

Pre-Construction Anti termite treatment is normally carried out before construction and Post-Construction Anti Termite Treatment is after construction.

It is a process where we treat the soil for termite control. Subterranean Termite can cause great losses to the interest of man especially in houses and buildings in urban areas. They also affect crops, habitation or utilities such as wooden poles. There have even been reports of termites damaging plastic or lead sheathings of underground cables.

We spray or inject termiticide to different stages of construction for pre constructed buildings and drill holes surrounding the buildings for post constructed. After treatment is carried out a service warranty of 5, 3, or 2 years was awarded to the client. Nowadays all government newly constructed projects have a stringent requirement to apply pre-construction anti termite treatment for its buildings.

Fumigation Services

Fumigation is the most effective way to force pests out from their harbourage and the use of poisonous gases can kill pests in an enclosed area. Fumigation gas generally does not leave unsightly, odorous, or hazardous residues. Fumigation will be handled by our licensed fumigator that is fully trained due to the extremely hazardous gases. The application of fumigants is limited to areas, spaces, items, or commodities that should be tightly enclosed.

Offshore Pest Control Services

Pest infestation can cause severe effect to the offshore industry which can affect human and the environment. The main problems with pests infestation on offshore gas, oil rigs, and offshore support vessels are can cause food contamination that causes food poisoning and also health problems and diseases to the. Besides that pest can cause damage to the structure. General pest control will be handled on offshore to control pest infestation which firstly our pest control specialist will identify the pest species, its habitat, and degree of infestation. Then Spraying, gelling, and baiting will be done depends on the pest identified. Next, placement of monitoring systems to further monitor the level of pest infestation, and recommend structural, sanitary and/or procedural modifications to reduce pests’ access to food, water and shelter.
