Health & Safety Policy, Accreditation and Certification

Health, Safety & Environment Policy Statement

It is the Policy on Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) of Pest Link that requires every employees to perform work in a manner that protects the health and safety of their colleagues, contractors and the general public, preserves the quality of the environment and prevents damage to property. It is also the requirement of the Policy on HSE to comply with the applicable laws, regulations, industry codes and practices.

Pest Link has established the following criteria in achieving the Policy objectives,

  • Health, Safety & Environment is prioritized at all levels of Pest Link business operations.
  • The health and safety of Pest Link employees, its customers, the public, including the impact on the environment, are critical factors that are taken into consideration in the planning and execution of all work activities undertaken by Pest Link.
  • Proactive implementation in identifying, eliminating or minimizing incident and environment exposures while taking into account technical, practical, social, economic and public-policy considerations.
  • To ensure all activities or processes are assessed in terms of its rick and to ensure appropriate risk control measures are in place.
  • All employees are communicated on their associated work hazards and be provided with sufficient control measures in accordance to the hierarchy controls.
  • Accomplishing efficient work processes by ensuring employees are medically-fit and competent through health and medical screening programs, instilling training and skill development; ensuring adequate supervision and awareness.
  • Keeping abreast of developing technologies and processes as well as statutory and mandatory requirements on HSE in the prevention of incidents/accidents and preservation of the environment.
  • Conduct independent audits and management review for verification of HSE status and the level of compliance.
  • Provide encouragement for proactive involvement of employees in all HSE activities and to recognize their HSE achievements.
